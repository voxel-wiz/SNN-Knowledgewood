SNN KNOWLEDGE WOOD

SNN Knowledge Wood is a place where children grow & develop while reading, listening and playing. Here they move from dependence to independence, from shaky coordination to refined skills, from body language to verbal communication, from self-absorption to growing social awareness. We provide the curriculum that promotes independence and explorations, provide both structured and free time, and involve all aspects of a child’s development through hands-on experience.
