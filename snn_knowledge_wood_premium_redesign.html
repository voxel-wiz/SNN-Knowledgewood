Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission process?

Our admission process involves filling out an online application form, followed by a school tour and a brief interaction session with the parents and child.

What are the school timings?

Timings vary by program. Pre-Montessori runs from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, while older grades operate from 8:30 AM to 2:30 PM.

Do you provide transport facilities?

Yes, we offer safe and secure transport facilities with GPS tracking and female attendants on all routes covering a 10km radius.
